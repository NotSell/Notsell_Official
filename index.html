Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to commonly asked questions

Not Sell Coin (NSC) is a cryptocurrency token with anti-dump mechanisms and automatic reward systems designed to encourage long-term holding and protect investors from high volatility.

The NSC smart contract implements progressive fees for large sales, limits on sale amounts per transaction, and delays for large sales, all designed to prevent dumping and protect token price.

Every transaction incurs a 5% fee that is distributed to all holders proportionally based on the amount of NSC held. The longer you hold, the more rewards you receive.
